responsibilities_html: >-

Project Leadership:

Lead and manage high-end residential design projects, overseeing all phases from conceptualization to construction administration.

Ensure projects adhere to the highest standards of design excellence, craftsmanship, and client satisfaction.

Design Innovation:

Infuse creativity and innovation into residential designs, integrating contemporary design principles with the rich cultural and architectural heritage of Bali.

Stay abreast of industry trends and incorporate cutting-edge design concepts into project deliverables.

Client Collaboration:

Collaborate closely with clients to understand their vision, lifestyle, and unique preferences.

Present design concepts and proposals in a compelling manner, incorporating client feedback to refine and enhance designs.

Team Collaboration:

Work collaboratively with internal design teams, providing guidance and support to ensure the successful execution of project visions.

Foster a collaborative and positive studio culture that values creativity, teamwork, and excellence.

Regulatory Compliance:

Ensure all projects comply with local building codes, regulations, and environmental standards.

Coordinate with relevant authorities and consultants to obtain necessary approvals.

Uphold an understanding and respect for Balinese culture, customs, and traditions to ensure that design concepts align with the local context and values.
